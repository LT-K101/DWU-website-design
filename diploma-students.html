Diploma Programs at DWU

Divine Word University offers a range of diploma programs designed to provide students with practical skills and knowledge for immediate employment or further study. Our diploma programs combine theoretical learning with hands-on experience to prepare students for success in their chosen fields.

Why Choose Our Diploma Programs?

  • Practical, industry-relevant curriculum
  • Experienced and qualified lecturers
  • Modern facilities and resources
  • Strong industry connections
  • Pathway to degree programs
  • Affordable tuition fees

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission into any diploma program, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Minimum Grade 12 certificate with a minimum GPA of 2.0
  • English proficiency (where applicable)
  • Completed application form
  • Certified copies of academic transcripts
  • Non-refundable application fee
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Medical examination report

How to Apply

1
Complete the Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.

2
Submit Documents

Upload all required documents including academic transcripts and identification.

3
Pay Application Fee

Complete the payment of the non-refundable application fee.

4
Interview (if required)

Attend an interview if requested by the admissions committee.

5
Receive Admission Letter

If accepted, you'll receive an official admission letter.

6
Complete Registration

Finalize your enrollment by completing the registration process.
